About Maysa

I am a Researcher and Master of Computer Science, currently working at Jusbrasil as a UX Researcher.

As a curious-minded person, I’m passionate about creating meaningful and ethical products. Experienced in mixed-methods studies but with a stronger emphasis on quantitative methods. My analytical and critical works always value inclusive and human-centred approaches while seeking in-depth understanding and uncovering opportunities. My research experience includes both industry and academia.

My latest project focused on creating HCI guidelines for researchers, designers and developers that want to explore the use of AI language models to assist individuals with complex communication needs. This work was developed in collaboration with the Virtual Reality and Multimedia Research Group (UFPE, Recife, Brazil) and the Inclusive Design Research Centre (OCAD, Toronto, Canada) as part of my master’s thesis.

Some of my research interests include Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Robotics, Trust and Privacy, Cybersecurity, Ubiquitous Computing, Data Science, Accessibility, and STEM Education.
